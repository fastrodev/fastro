# Overview

**Fragmen** is reimagining employee attendance for the age of remote and asynchronous work. Unlike traditional systems that tether employees to specific "9-to-5" windows, Fragmen empowers employees to work when they are most productive.

The core philosophy is simple: **Work when you want, provided you meet your daily duration goal.** This is perfect for remote teams where work-life balance and deep-work sessions are prioritized over rigid scheduling.

# Core Features

The proposed system focuses on flexibility and cumulative tracking:

1.  **Multi-Session Check-In/Out** — Employees can start and stop their work multiple times throughout the day (e.g., 2 hours in the morning, 4 hours in the afternoon, 2 hours at night).
2.  **Daily Duration Tracking** — Instead of "late" or "early," the system tracks progress toward a target daily duration (e.g., 8 hours).
3.  **Async-First Dashboard** — Managers see cumulative hours worked for the day rather than just a "presence" status, providing better insight into team capacity.
4.  **Flexible Scheduling** — No fixed start or end times. Employees work according to their preferences and time zones.
5.  **Location Independence** — Designed for remote work with optional verification that focuses on meaningful progress rather than physical presence.

# Proposed Workflow

1.  **Start Session** — Employee clicks "Check-In" whenever they start a task.
2.  **Pause/Stop Session** — Employee clicks "Check-Out" for breaks, errands, or ending their session. 
3.  **Cumulative Progress** — The system aggregates all sessions for the day into a total "Hours Worked" counter.
4.  **Goal Reached** — Notifications or visual indicators show when the employee has met their daily commitment.
